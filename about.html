I am a freelance designer living and working in Berlin.

Hi, my name is Kristina. You might know me as Kriesse, which is my alter ego on the web. As a freelance designer, my services include art direction and illustration as well as developing complex frontend designs for the web. I have a wide range of skills to facilitate my work and have specialized in coding (X)HTML/CSS. Naturally, I am a huge fan of web standards and accessibility. I like my code clean and minimal, and so is my ideal of design.

Right now I am booked solid, but nevertheless on the lookout for future challenges. I am especially interested in working for clients in the arts and culture field. If you need advice in crafting an innovative website, want a strong visual concept for your exhibition catalogue or run a magazine in need of fresh illustrations, do not hesitate to get in touch.
